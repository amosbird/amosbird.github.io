Autohotkey, Thinkpad saviour

2015-09-20

Trackpoint driver sucks

Have you ever felt that scrolling function is broken on Thinkpad? If so, you must try this script.

With this AHK script, trackpoint behaves normally. This is not the end of the story. Autohotkey is much more powerful. Suppose you have a email account named helloworld@gmail.com, just type h@ and let AHK finish for you. Not to mention remapping AltTab to relieve your pinkies.
